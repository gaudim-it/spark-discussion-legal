Privacy Policy for Spark Discussion

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Spark Discussion ("the App") is a free mobile application created by New Beginning Apps as an ad-supported service.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, our third-party ad partner Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads, including your device's IP address, device identifiers, and usage data.

Third Party Services

The App uses the following third party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob
  • Expo

Log Data

In the event of an error in the App, third party services may collect data known as Log Data, which may include your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, and the time and date of your use of the App.

Cookies

The App does not use cookies directly. However, third party services used by the App may use cookies to collect information and improve their services.

Data Retention

The App stores your saved favourite questions locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
